What is Chess.AI?
A Machine Learning application wherein Chess enthusiasts can practice with AI trained against the data of professional players (i.e., Magnus Carlsen) or amateurs within set Elo rating ranges; ChessAI strives to be a practice tool that will challenge players, transforming them into stronger opponents.
Chess.AI is a project developed by Callum Organ as part of his undergraduate disseration throughout Sep. 2022 - June 2023.
